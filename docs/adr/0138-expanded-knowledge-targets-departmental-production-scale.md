# Expanded Knowledge Targets Departmental Production Scale

Accepted.

[FRAME | HIGH] The expanded insurance Knowledge architecture targets one Knowledge Source containing 500 through 10,000 long PDF documents and approximately 100,000 through 2,000,000 pages over the next two to three years. The current 500-document foundation capacity and routing-model projection of at most 100 document candidates remain current-state constraints rather than the target design. Ingestion must rebuild only affected immutable artifacts, and query-time retrieval must use bounded indexed filtering and ranking rather than scan every document or expose the complete catalog to a model. We accept a more capable catalog and retrieval layer because encoding pilot volume into the long-term architecture would force a disruptive replacement as the departmental corpus grows.
