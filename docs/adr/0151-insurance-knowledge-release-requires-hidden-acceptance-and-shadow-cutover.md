# Insurance Knowledge Release Requires Hidden Acceptance And Shadow Cutover

Accepted.

[FRAME | HIGH] The expanded insurance Knowledge path is released through an Insurance Knowledge Gold Suite of 500 human-confirmed business-query cases, split into 300 visible tuning cases and 200 access-controlled unseen acceptance cases while preserving the agreed 30-percent clause-lookup, 50-percent conditional-guidance, and 20-percent comparison profile. A sealed evaluator permits one acceptance attempt per frozen production candidate and withholds case-level feedback; any revealed failed case moves to tuning and is replenished before the next acceptance decision. A separate 100-to-200-sample parsing benchmark covers layout, table, OCR, reading-order, review detection, and citation-coordinate fidelity and keeps an access-controlled acceptance slice. The Insurance Knowledge Hard Release Gate is non-compensating: unauthorized candidate exposure, wrong-version or wrong-precedence use, unresolvable formal citations, advice under unresolved authority, and high-severity unsupported conclusions must each remain at zero in the unseen cohort. Initial quality targets are required-evidence Recall@50 of at least 95 percent overall and 90 percent per query-type slice, complete-evidence Top-10 coverage of at least 90 percent for conditional-guidance and comparison cases, hybrid retrieval plus reranking P95 including GPU queue time at or below five seconds under the initial five-active-run envelope, and no more than ten-percent online-latency regression during ingestion. The one-to-four-hour routine activation objective applies only inside a measured Routine Knowledge Change Workload Envelope and ends after the named target Agent is published and active. Rollout uses Knowledge Shadow Cutover through parser shadow, OpenSearch dual retrieval, assisted pilot, gated activation, immediate Agent Version Rollback, and separately governed Source rollback Drafts rather than silent TreeIndex fallback. We accept additional curation, measurement, and staged operations so model selection cannot tune on its release exam and aggregate quality cannot conceal authority or authorization failure.
