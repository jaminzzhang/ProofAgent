# Production Knowledge Processing Remains Inside Company Trust Boundary

Accepted.

[FRAME | HIGH] Production Knowledge document originals and every content-bearing derivative, including parsed text, recovered tables and images, OCR output, chunks, embeddings, indexes, retrieval and reranker inputs, Candidate or Accepted Evidence content, and final-answer evidence context, remain inside the company-controlled Private Knowledge Processing Boundary. External SaaS services are therefore not eligible for this processing path merely because their origins could be added to Production Egress Policy; approved internal services may still use explicit governed network paths. We accept the additional infrastructure and model-operations cost because allowing only originals to remain internal would leave substantively equivalent business content exposed through derivatives and model prompts.
