# Initial Production Support Is Five by Eight

Accepted.

[FRAME | HIGH] The initial internal private pilot has a business-day support window from 09:00 through 18:00 in `Asia/Shanghai`. During that window, a P0 incident involving service unavailability, a security event, or material data-loss risk requires acknowledgement within 30 minutes; a P1 incident involving major degradation of a core capability requires acknowledgement within four hours. Outside the window, response is best effort and the initial release makes no 24-by-7 on-call or external support SLA commitment.

[FRAME | HIGH] The 99.0% monthly availability SLO continues to measure the service across the full calendar month. Unsupported hours do not become an availability exclusion; alerts, incident records, rollback evidence, and follow-up reviews remain required even when acknowledgement occurs in the next support window.
