# Insurance Rule Applicability And Precedence Are Deterministic

Accepted.

[FRAME | HIGH] Before an Institution Specialist Response Projection synthesizes product terms, underwriting rules, sales rules, or scoped exceptions, the governed Knowledge path determines Insurance Rule Applicability and applies a business-approved Insurance Rule Precedence Order. Retrieval rank, semantic similarity, filename recency, and model judgment cannot establish rule authority. The model may explain the resulting basis and expose assumptions or conflicts, but unresolved applicability or precedence produces an explicit source-conflict result requiring staff confirmation rather than an invented reconciliation. We accept the metadata curation and governance cost because probabilistic ranking is not a valid substitute for business rule authority.
