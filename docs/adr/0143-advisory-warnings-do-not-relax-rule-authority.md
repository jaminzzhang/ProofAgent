# Advisory Warnings Do Not Relax Rule Authority

Accepted.

[FRAME | HIGH] The Institution Insurance Specialist may use a balanced answer policy only after Insurance Rule Authority Gate proves authorized visibility, the published rule version, Insurance Rule Applicability, Insurance Rule Precedence Order, and valid citation. Once that gate passes, bounded uncertainty about evidence completeness or interpretation may appear as an Advisory Evidence Warning in an Evidence-Bound Specialist Recommendation. Authority uncertainty instead produces clarification, source conflict, or no-recommendation behavior; warning text cannot convert an unverified rule into a probable answer. We accept some additional refusals to prevent a user-visible caveat from disguising an authorization or business-authority failure.
