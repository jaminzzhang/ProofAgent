# Insurance Rule Authority Metadata Requires Business Approval

Accepted.

[FRAME | HIGH] Insurance rule scope, effective period, authority, precedence, and supersession facts currently derived from PDFs and supporting spreadsheets enter Proof Agent only as an Insurance Rule Metadata Draft, whether extracted deterministically, proposed by a model, or loaded through Insurance Rule Metadata Workbook Import. Workbook rows use a versioned non-executable template, bind to exact document revisions and reviewed structural anchors, preserve original and normalized artifacts, and expose field conflicts rather than resolving PDF and workbook disagreement automatically. A business reviewer must confirm or correct those facts before they become Approved Insurance Rule Metadata, and only approved metadata may participate in Insurance Rule Applicability, Insurance Rule Precedence Order, source publication validation, or a published Knowledge version. Model extraction and workbook import cannot approve their own output, and changing approved authority metadata requires a new immutable Insurance Rule Unit Revision plus renewed validation before publication. We accept the curation workload because unreviewed extraction or spreadsheet errors would otherwise become deterministic but false business authority.

[FRAME | HIGH] Approval must construct and validate the strict Approved Insurance Rule Metadata Revision contract; a review cannot become publishable merely because its two drafts contain the same missing required value. Approved and rejected reviews are immutable terminal decisions until a separately governed revocation workflow exists. Workbook imports record the trusted operator and aware timestamp and append trace-safe configuration audit references. Review listing uses bounded opaque-cursor pages with a server-computed global approval summary, so publication readiness never depends on which page the browser currently displays.

[FRAME | HIGH] Each Source maintains a rebuildable, generation-fenced Review Index containing only ordered review identity, import, state, version, and publication-blocking facts. Page reads load that index and deserialize at most the selected page of full Review records. Review records, index generations, and immutable decision entries commit behind one cross-process lock and a shared durable redo journal; normal write failures roll all three projections back, while an interrupted rollback is completed before the next locked reader can observe state. Deterministic workbook replays preserve the original importer and timestamp, return an explicit replay marker, and idempotently ensure the exact import mutation audit: a missing audit is repaired, an exact audit is retained, and conflicting audit facts fail closed.

[FRAME | HIGH] Hybrid completion persists its exact artifact result using atomic replacement plus file and directory durability barriers. An invalid partial result may be quarantined and rebuilt only while the caller holds the live exact ingestion claim and after all referenced artifacts and metadata projections validate; a stale claim cannot repair or replace completion state. XLSX preflight applies platform-independent OPC containment checks to archive members and relationship targets, rejecting backslash, drive, UNC, traversal, uncontained, executable, and external-link paths before workbook parsing.
