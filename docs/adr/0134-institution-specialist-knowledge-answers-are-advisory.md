# Institution Specialist Knowledge Answers Are Advisory

Accepted.

[FRAME | HIGH] In Assisted Service Mode, Knowledge-backed synthesis may produce an Evidence-Bound Specialist Recommendation with a concise advisory conclusion, governed source basis, assumptions, conflicts, missing information, and citations, but institution staff must confirm it before communication or action. The Agent does not issue formal underwriting eligibility, sales authorization, coverage, payment, or other executable insurance decisions. If a future release requires such authority, it must introduce a separately governed, versioned business-rule or decision-service boundary; RAG or model synthesis cannot acquire that authority through prompt configuration. We accept less straight-through automation to preserve human accountability and prevent probabilistic document interpretation from masquerading as an operational decision.
