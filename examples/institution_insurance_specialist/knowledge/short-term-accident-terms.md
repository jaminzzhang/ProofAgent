# Short-Term Accident Terms

Short-term accident products generally provide coverage for accidental injury
events during a bounded policy period. Coverage interpretation must be grounded
in the product wording, endorsements, exclusions, and the applicable operational
rules.

For public or generic questions, the Agent may explain clause structure,
common exclusions, required materials, and service process terms from approved
knowledge. It must not decide whether a specific claim is payable without
authorized claim records and human business review.

If policy wording and a live business record appear to conflict, the source
authority order applies: live records describe current state, while wording and
operational documents explain rules. The response should call out the conflict
instead of reconciling it.
