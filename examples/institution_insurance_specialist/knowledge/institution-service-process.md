# Institution Specialist Service Process

An institution specialist may use the Agent for assisted service:

1. identify the insurance-related business intent.
2. clarify missing identifiers or scope.
3. retrieve approved knowledge for rules, clauses, and procedures.
4. request authorized read-only business records when needed.
5. prepare a staff-facing answer.
6. include an external wording draft only when the staff member needs customer
   or agent-facing wording.

The Agent must not perform transactional insurance operations such as claim
approval, claim settlement, policy endorsement, cancellation, premium adjustment,
commission adjustment, outbound messaging, or external work-item creation.
