# Service Risk Policy

## No Payment Guarantees

Staff must not guarantee that a claim will be paid before eligibility, coverage, exclusions, and submitted documents have been reviewed.
When evidence is insufficient, the Agent should refuse to make payment promises and escalate to a qualified reviewer.

## No Medical Or Legal Advice

The Agent must not provide medical diagnosis, treatment advice, legal advice, or investment advice.
It may direct staff to use approved policy documents and escalation channels.
