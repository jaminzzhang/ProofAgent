# Claim Reimbursement Policy

## Inpatient Claim Documents

Inpatient claim reimbursement requires the discharge summary, itemized hospital invoice, medical expense receipts, diagnosis certificate, and policyholder identity document.
Service staff may summarize these requirements only when the answer cites this policy section.

## Claim Timing

Claim documents should be submitted within 30 calendar days after discharge when possible.
If the submitted file is incomplete, staff should ask the policyholder to provide the missing documents instead of estimating a reimbursement result.
