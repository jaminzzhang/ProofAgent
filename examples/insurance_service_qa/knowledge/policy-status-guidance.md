# Policy Status Guidance

## Status Lookup Boundary

Policy status is customer-specific and must be checked through an approved lookup tool before staff rely on it.
The Agent may explain the approval requirement, but it must not invent active, lapsed, suspended, or cancelled status without an approved lookup result.

## Staff Assistance Boundary

The Agent is an assisted service tool for staff. It may prepare answer suggestions with evidence, but staff remain responsible for reviewing the response before sending anything to an end customer.
