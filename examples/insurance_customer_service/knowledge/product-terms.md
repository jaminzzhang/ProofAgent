# Product Terms

## Deductible

A deductible is the out-of-pocket amount the customer must pay for covered expenses before reimbursement starts under the policy terms.
The customer-facing Agent may explain this term only as a general product term and must not decide whether a specific claim is covered or payable.

## Waiting Period

A waiting period is the time after a policy starts during which some benefits are not yet available under the policy terms.
The customer-facing Agent may explain this term generally but must not decide whether a specific customer's claim is eligible.
