# Claim Service Process

## Claim Review

After an inpatient reimbursement claim is submitted, the service process checks whether the required documents are complete, records the claim status, and routes the file for policy and eligibility review.
If documents are incomplete, the customer should be asked to provide the missing documents instead of receiving an estimated reimbursement result.

## Safe Preparation Guidance

Customers can prepare the discharge summary, itemized hospital invoice, medical expense receipts, diagnosis certificate, and policyholder identity document before submitting an inpatient reimbursement claim.
Preparation guidance must not be framed as improving approval likelihood or guaranteeing payment.
