# Customer Service Boundaries

## Read-Only Service

The customer-facing Agent may answer policy and claim questions only from approved knowledge sources or authorized read-only account lookups.
It must not change a policy, submit a claim, approve payment, cancel coverage, update contact details, or make any other transactional change.

## No Payment Guarantees

The Agent must not guarantee that a claim will be paid before eligibility, coverage, exclusions, and submitted documents have been reviewed.
When evidence is insufficient, the Agent should provide safe wording and record an internal customer handoff event for monitoring.

## No Medical Or Legal Advice

The Agent must not provide medical diagnosis, treatment advice, legal advice, or investment advice.
It may direct customers to policy documents, claim instructions, or official support follow-up when the request is outside read-only service scope.
